About

Our ISO 9001 certification confirms that the business and operational processes we use satisfy this international quality standard. The management system documents how we do business and provides staff with the opportunity to continually question why and how we do what we do, and whether there is a better way. It is this approach that will help us to enhance our reputation in the market place and exceed our Clients' expectations.
Our AS/NZS 4801 / BS 18001 certification for the Occupational Health & Safety Management System (OHS-MS) means that we have satisfied the national standard for the implementation of a systematic approach to managing OHS. This system enables us to meet our legal requirements and lead to a sustained improvement in OHS performance. The success of the OHS-MS depends upon the commitment from all levels and functions within the Company.
Our ISO 14001 certification for the Environmental Management System (EMS) means that we have satisfied the national standard for the implementation of a systematic approach to managing the environment. The EMS enables the company to meet our environmental policy objectives of taking into account the environmental issues in all of our operations and endeavor to minimize waste and prevent pollution.
